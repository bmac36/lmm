Get to know us better

What is Kindermusik?

Kindermusik provides weekly classes of music and movement activities for parents and their children, newborn to age 7. It provides families with opportunities to interact more effectively, instills a deep appreciation for music, enriches all areas of development, and focuses on the process of "learning how to learn", preparing the brain and body for all future learning.

In a Kindermusik class, our educators will lead you and other parents, together with their children, through joyful activities, using music and movement. You will learn more about your child's unique developmental process. The shared learning experience creates a unique bond as your child will associate learning with fun, musical play.

For 25 years, Kindermusik International has been developing curricula based on new and proven research in child development. Award-winning books, beautifully-produced music, imaginative games, and authentic musical instruments, nurture skills using a holistic and developmentally appropriate approach, addressing the needs of the whole child.

The learning process continues at home, through the use of carefully planned resource materials designed to encourage family movement and dance activities to improve coordination and balance, one-on-one parent and child interaction to nurture self-esteem, and music-making and music listening activities to develop self-discipline, as well as critical and creative thinking skills.

Meet the Team

I am pleased to present our Studio Staff. I search diligently for teachers who not only have a love for children, but who have a natural ability to relate to both children and adults. Sherri Stuebing Director

Sherri has been involved with music all of her life, and has loved the opportunity to her passion for music onto her own children and the children that she teaches. With an early start to music at the age of 3, when her father taught her "Twinkle Twinkle" on the piano, she fell in love with music, and musical theatre and still continues to be active in the musical community, participating in the Annual Kitchener Rock Revival and other local arts events. Sherri has a B.A in English and a minor in Music Education, has taught piano and flute, and was assistant choral director for the Kitchener-Waterloo Sweet Adeline Ladies Barbershop Chorus from 2003 – 2008.

Her two talented children have attended Kindermusik since birth. Isabella continues to carry her love of music into her competitive dancing and musical theatre. As a mom and a teacher of Kindermusik, Sherri believes that this is the perfect program for young children to start their musical education in a fun and nurturing way. She is thrilled to take over as director of this fabulous program!
