Private lessons for all ages

Private Lessons

Do you have a budding musician in your family? Are you looking for piano,voice,guitar, or violin/fiddle lessons?

Little Music Makerz offers private lessons out of two locations:

  • 248 Highland Rd E Kitchener
  • 25 Bridgeport Rd E Waterloo

Currently booking for lesson times for the school year. For more information please email littlemusicmakerz@gmail.com or call 519-577-6121.

Cost: $30.00 per lesson. Term runs for 32 weeks. Payment installments available for full term bookings.

Our Private Lesson Programs

  • Preschool Prodigies Early Introduction to Voice

    Learn vocal production basics, solfege signs and basic ear training through a fun curricula that uses games and listening activities to encourage voice finding and proper technique.

  • Little Music Makerz Beginner and Elementary Piano

    Learn the rudimentary basic of piano playing through technique, theory and performance. Learn helpful performance tips and gain confidence in playing for others.

  • Music Makerz II Piano for the Advanced Learner

    Continue your training with our music writing and composition expert. Learn how to create your own masterpieces through chord study, jazz riffs, improvisation and traditional pedagogy.

  • Violin for the Beginner

    Our violin instructors can provide contemporary fiddle or classical violin lessons. Even bluegrass! Teachers work with students on a range of subjects such as music theory, sight-reading, and improvisation.

  • Adult Piano Lessons available from Beginner to advanced level

    Learn to play in a low-stress environment. Our instructors embrace the philosophy of compehensive musicianship to include basic music knowledge, theory, and piano technique using a variety of musical styles. Adult students may prefer to have their lessons more narrowly designed around preferred musical styles such as classical, pop/rock, New Age, improvisation, sacred and more.

  • Wunderkeys Preschool and Beginner Piano Program

    We offer a unique blend of music and imagination that will nourish the intellectual and creative minds of your child. This one-on-one piano program, that will keep your preschooler excited, motivated, and begging to learn more, as they explore music through games and math concepts.

  • Music Makerz I Piano for the Intermediate learner

    Explore more than method books in this exciting program which focuses on theory, composition, improvisation and ear-training, as well as confidence in playing for performance. RCM repertoire and examinations are also an option.

  • Beginner voice lessons

    Learn the basics of vocal technique through exercises, solfege study and sight reading. Sing popular songs, musical theatre, classical or folk songs while gaining confidence in singing in front of others and performing.

  • Guitar for all ages

    Our guitar teacher will help you learn to read music, improve technique, and develop an in-depth understanding of what it means to be a musician. Beginner to Advanced levels.
