Welcome to Our Studio!

With more than 35 years of experience as the leader in musical learning, Kindermusik International understands music’s unique ability to impact children (and families!) in profound ways. Parents also appreciate how enrollment in Kindermusik includes home materials, including their favorite songs, stories, and learning activities from class, so families can “play” Kindermusik wherever they go.

Little Music Makerz presents Kindermusik is has been serving the Kitchener and surrounding areas since 1996. We have two large classrooms, A spacious waiting area and a kitchen for your convenience. We are dedicated to making scheduling easy for our families, so we try our best to run concurrent classes which allows you to spend less time at the studio and more time at home singing, playing and bonding with your family. Our studio educators are certified, trained and licensed Kindermusik instructors who love bringing the world of Kindermusik to our community. With strong backgrounds in music training as well as a vast knowledge in child development, our team is thrilled to help you and your child to learn, play, laugh and discover the world of music together.

Each week instructors lead music classes for families filled with singing, dancing, laughing, instrument playing, and storytelling—all backed by Kindermusik International’s team of early childhood music experts and the latest research on how children learn best. Kindermusik educators believe that that you are your child’s best teacher. We partner with parents to support this very special role by providing tools, tips, and resources to understand how (and why!) to use music and movement throughout the week, so the learning continues at home.

Little Music Makerz presents Kindermusik has achieved the status of Kindermusik Maestro program, soaring into the top 5% of Kindermusik programs worldwide.

Come experience for yourself why more than 2 million families in over 70 countries love Kindermusik.
