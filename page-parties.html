Kindermusik Birthday Parties

Looking for a unique and FUN way to celebrate your little one’s special day? A Kindermusik® Birthday Party is the perfect solution!

We’re the EXPERTS when it comes to making music and making children smile! Invite us to your child’s next birthday party and EVERYONE—adults and children alike—will enjoy a delightful musical celebration!

Our Kindermusik Birthday Parties are completely interactive and enjoyable for children and adults! We can help alleviate some of the stress of birthday party planning and entertaining by kicking off your event with musical fun and entertainment.

Each package includes instrument exploration, instrument play-along, creative movement, story time, and circle dances. We even provide a gift for the birthday child! So... set the date, check out the birthday party package info below, and contact us to schedule your musical celebration or to find out more.

We offer two birthday party packages:

Package A: Studio Location

“Musical Celebrations”

  • 45 minutes of music and movement led by a certified Kindermusik educator
  • Table and facilities for birthday snacks/cake (client provided)
  • 2 hour room rental
  • Small gift for the birthday child

Cost: $180.00

Package B: At Your Home*

“Musical Adventures”

  • 45 minutes of music and movement led by a certified Kindermusik educator
  • 15 minute craft
  • Small gift for the birthday child

Cost: $165.00

*A travel fee of $15.00 may be required if venue exceeds 25 km from the studio location.

Make your child’s special day extra special with these extra offerings:

Have more questions? Check out the Kindermusik® Birthday FAQ…

How many children can attend and what are the recommended ages for a birthday party?

We recommend a minimum of 7 and a maximum of 12 children in attendance at the party. A Kindermusik Birthday Party is best suited for children between the ages of 6 months and 7 year. For children and guests ages 1 to 3, there must be one actively participating adult with each child. For children over the age of 3, adult participation is encouraged, but optional. We do ask that at least ONE adult be present during the music and movement portion.

Where does Kindermusik® fit into the party schedule?

Plan to start your party with Kindermusik. Food and gifts should come out after the musical fun!

Where do Kindermusik birthday parties take place?

Parties can be held in the Studio or at your home. If we are traveling to your home, we’ll supply the instruments, props, CD player, and the entertainment but we will require a large open space for the musical fun. NOTE: The space must be large enough for all in attendance to sit and stand in a circle as well as to move around freely. Indoor space preferred.

When/how are parties scheduled?

Parties are scheduled on demand and first come first serve. Parties begin and end promptly as scheduled. At least 2 weeks’ notice is required to ensure the party fits on our calendar.

Can I customize the birthday party package?

Absolutely! We have a number of available themes but are willing to incorporate something special if requested. We are able to customize our activities to your chosen theme.
